✦ Our Story

About The Repairers

A ministry devoted to restoring lives, rebuilding faith, and spreading the Word of God freely across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

Rebuilding Lives Through the Power of God's Word

The Repairers is a Christian ministry built on the conviction that the Gospel is the greatest force for restoration in the world. We believe that every broken life, every wounded spirit, and every searching soul can be made whole through an encounter with Jesus Christ.

Through GraceWave, our digital platform, we make anointed sermons, life-changing books, and in-depth Bible study materials freely available to every person — regardless of where they are or what they can afford.

Our name comes from Isaiah 58:12 — a call to be those who repair the broken places, restore the ancient foundations, and raise up a generation that walks in the fullness of God's purpose.

✝
"And they that shall be of thee shall build the old waste places: thou shalt raise up the foundations of many generations; and thou shalt be called, The repairer of the breach..."
— Isaiah 58:12 (KJV)
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

📖
Word-Centred
Everything we do is rooted in the Bible. We believe the Word of God is living, active, and sufficient for every need of the human soul.
🙏
Prayer & Faith
We are a praying ministry. We believe that prayer is the engine of every move of God, and we are committed to a lifestyle of faith and intercession.
🌍
Free For All
The Gospel is free. Every sermon, book, and study note on GraceWave is available at no cost — because the grace of God cannot be bought or sold.
❤️
Love & Compassion
We serve people with the love of Christ — meeting them where they are, walking with them in their journey, and pointing them always to the Saviour.
🔥
Holy Spirit Led
We do not lean on human wisdom alone. Every message, every resource, every decision is submitted to the leading of the Holy Spirit.
🏗️
Building People
Our goal is not just converts but disciples — men and women who are grounded in the Word, filled with the Spirit, and sent to impact their world.
Our Journey

How GraceWave Was Born

The Repairers began with a simple but powerful burden — to make the Word of God accessible to as many people as possible in Nigeria and beyond. We saw how many believers hungered for quality Christian content but couldn't always access it.

GraceWave was created as the digital arm of that vision. A place where anyone, anywhere, at any time, can download a sermon that will strengthen their faith, a book that will transform their thinking, or a Bible study that will deepen their walk with God.

We are not a large organisation. We are a community of believers who love God, love His Word, and love His people — and we are committed to serving the body of Christ with every resource God entrusts to us.
