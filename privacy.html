🙏 Our commitment: GraceWave is a free Christian resource platform run by The Repairers Ministry. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling any personal information with care, transparency, and integrity.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how GraceWave ("we", "us", or "our"), the digital platform of The Repairers Ministry, collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website and download our content.

By using GraceWave, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the website.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience possible. This includes:

  • Information you provide voluntarily — such as your name and email address if you subscribe to our newsletter or send us a message via the contact form.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you use the site, such as which pages you visit and which files you download. This helps us understand what content is most useful to our visitors.
  • Device information — your browser type, operating system, and IP address, collected automatically when you visit our site.

We do not collect payment information, as all our content is completely free.

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the GraceWave platform and its content
  • Respond to your enquiries or prayer requests sent via the contact form
  • Send you new content updates if you subscribe to our newsletter (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Analyse usage patterns to understand which sermons and books are most helpful to our community

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — We use Supabase to store and manage our content database and files. They process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure, which is hosted on industry-standard cloud servers. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

GraceWave uses minimal cookies necessary for the website to function properly. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Your browser settings can be used to refuse cookies if you prefer, though this may affect some features of the site.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected].

Children's Privacy

GraceWave is a general Christian resource platform suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.
